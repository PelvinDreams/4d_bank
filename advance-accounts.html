Are you eligible?
  • You must pay at least $1,750 a month into the account (or a minimum of $10,500 every 6 months), not including money transferred from other sole or joint personal accounts held with Not A Bank US
  • You need to be 18 years or older
  • Your residential address must be within the EU
  • You must be able to provide us with certified proof of your identity and address (translated into English if appropriate)
  • If you're resident in the US, you're happy for us to undertake a credit register search (PDF)
