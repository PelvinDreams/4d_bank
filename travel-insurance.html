Cover to suit your every need while travelling

Not A Bank Travel Insurance

Not A Bank Travel Insurance is designed to offer a choice of single trip or annual multi-trip cover.

  • Up to £10 million of medical cover

    • For emergency medical treatment and associated expenses

  • Up to £5000 cancellation cover

    • Refund of non-recoverable travel and accommodation costs should you be forced to cancel or cut short your holiday

  • Cover for airspace or port closures

    • If you are prevented from travelling or stranded abroad for at least 24 consecutive hours due to airspace or port closures, you'll be covered

  • 15% online discount

    • Receive a 15% online discount when you buy your single trip or annual multi-trip policy online. Offer only available on new policies.

  • Cover for major incidents

    • If you need to cancel or abandon your trip due to major incident such as a terror attack, you'll be covered.

  • Up to 9 people covered

    • Ideal for solo travellers, couples, families and groups

  • Optional extras

    • To cover baggage, extended trip, scuba diving, golf and winter sports

  • Choice of single trip or annual multi-trip policies

    • With UK only, European or Worldwide cover available

What's covered?

  • Pre-existing medical conditions of any travellers named on the policy and anyone whose health may affect your decision to take or continue your trip unless accepted in writing by us
  • Leisure activities not listed in the policy document
  • Missed international departure due to strike if the date of strike is known at the time of booking
  • Winter sports above the 31 days maximum stated on annual policies
  • Business trips which involve manual work or dealing with the public
  • Claims for your death, injury, illness or disability are not covered if they result from you misusing alcohol or drugs.

Please note, all trips must begin and end in the UK, Channel Islands or Isle of Man. Holidays in the UK, Channel Islands or Isle of Man must include a minimum of 2 consecutive nights stay in pre booked accommodation.

Documents you may need when making a travel insurance claim

  • Original receipts, bills, cash withdrawal slips, flight and luggage tickets, guarantees, instruction manuals, accident reports, any photographs of your belongings and medical certificates or reports
  • A written police report made as soon as reasonably possible for any theft or loss
  • A "Property Irregularity Report" for baggage lost or damaged by an airline
  • Written confirmation from your tour operator or carrier to validate any delays

Please note, this is not a complete list as each claim is different. Please read your policy document for full details.

Are you eligible?

Please ensure you can answer YES to the following questions:

  • Are you aged between 16 and 79 for a Single Trip policy or aged between 16 and 74 for an Annual Multi-trip policy?
  • Have you been resident in the UK, Channel Islands or Isle of Man for the last six months?
  • Are you liable to pay tax in the UK, Channel Islands or Isle of Man and are you registered with a local doctor?

To ensure you avoid having duplicate cover, please check whether you or any traveller who would be covered by this policy have any other products that currently provide travel insurance.

Before applying, please also take the time to read important information on the policy.
