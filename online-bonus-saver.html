Online bonus saver

Savings, with an extra reward from us

Not A Bank Online Bonus Saver

Earn bonus interest every month you don't make a withdrawal.

  • Start saving with just £1

    • That's all you need to open an account, to a maximum of £2,000,000. Please note that accounts are limited to one per customer

  • Get a higher rate on balances of up to £50,000

    • If you have more than £50,000 in your account, we pay a lower interest rate on the amount above that – see our interest rates.

  • Bonus interest, calculated daily

    • When you qualify for a bonus interest payment, it’s calculated on the daily balance and paid monthly.

  • Manage your account online

    • As the name suggests, this account is ‘online only’. That means you can access your funds anytime you need them and all your interactions with this account will be online.

How to apply

Open a Online Bonus Saver today

Please read the important product information and check eligibility before you apply.

Key product information

How it works

    Our Online Bonus Saver account gives you a variable rate of 0.45% AER/gross (including bonus interest) for any calendar month you don't make a withdrawal. You'll get a variable rate of 0.10% AER/gross in any calendar month you do make a withdrawal or close your account.

    You can open an Online Bonus Saver with £1 or more. If you have more than £50,000 in your account, interest is paid at a lower rate on balances above that amount.

Question

What is the account name?

OnlineBonusSaver

What is the interest rate?

Standard Rate

Interest rate on first £50,000: 0.10% AER (0.10% Gross)
Interest rate on any balance over £50,000: 0.10% AER (0.10% Gross)

Interest Rate including bonus

Interest rate on first £50,000: 0.45% AER (0.45% Gross)
Interest rate on any balance over £50,000: 0.25% AER (0.25% Gross)

Interest is calculated daily and credited to the account monthly.

The standard rate of interest is paid in any month where a withdrawal is made or you close your account.

The interest rate including bonus is paid in any month where you don't make a withdrawal or don't close your account.

Balances up to £50,000 will earn the higher rate of interest. Only the part of the balance above £50,000 will earn the lower rate of interest.

Can Not A Bank change the interest rate?

Yes, the interest rate is variable. We can change the interest rate on this account, in line with the account's Terms and Conditions.

How do I open & manage my account?

Eligibility:

  • Aged 16 years or over
  • Able to manage your account online only

How to open the account:

  • Open an account via Online Banking only

Minimum/Maximum Balance:

  • A minimum of £1 must be deposited to open the account
  • The maximum balance you can hold in the account is £2,000,000

How to manage the account

  • Once opened the account can be managed via Online Banking or the Not A Bank UK Mobile Banking App

Can I withdraw money?

Yes, you have instant access to your account. You can make as many fee free withdrawals as you like. However if you do make a withdrawal you will receive the standard rate of interest for that month.

Additional Information

A 14 day cancellation period applies to this account.

We do not deduct tax from any interest paid to you.

Tax benefits depend on individual circumstances and could change in the future.

Definitions

  • AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate. This shows you what the gross rate would be if interest were paid and compounded each year.
  • Gross is the rate of interest if interest were paid and not compounded each year

Rates correct as at 06.04.18

Are you eligible?

  • able to operate the account solely online through online banking

Register for online banking

Non-UK tax residents

If you’re liable to pay tax outside the UK, please apply at your local branch.
