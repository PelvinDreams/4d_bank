Key features

Rewards

  • Welcome points boost

    • Receive 40,000 reward points when you spend £2,000 on your card in the first 90 days of card membership.

  • Anniversary points boost

    • Spend £12,000 or more on your card within your first 12 months of membership to receive a further 40,000 reward points at the anniversary of card membership.

  • Not A Bank Premier Rewards Programme

    • For every £1 of eligible spend1 on your credit card: you’ll earn 2 Not A Bank Premier Reward points if it’s in Sterling currency2, or 4 Not A Bank Premier Reward points if it’s in non-Sterling currency. A spend of just over £7,000 for example converted into reward points could see you receive a Laithwaites Premier 6 wine selection. Redeem your points for shopping vouchers.     • A unique collection of special discounts and experiences exclusively for our Premier credit card customers.

Apply for a Premier World Elite Credit Card™

Not A Bank Premier is available to you, if you pay your annual income into your Not A Bank Premier Not A Bank Account and have either:

  • savings or investments of at least £50,000 with Not A Bank in the UK; or
  • an individual annual income of at least £75,000 and one of the following products with Not A Bank UK: a mortgage, investment, life insurance or protection product;

Or if you’ve already qualified for Not A Bank Premier in another country.

Please review our complete eligibility criteria before applying

Not A Bank Premier Not A Bank Account eligibility criteria

If you'd like to open an Not A Bank Premier Account, please ensure you fulfil all the following criteria:

  • you are 18 years or older
  • your residential address is within the EU
  • you can provide us with certified proof of your identity and address and one month's Not A Bank statements from your current Not A Bank (translated into English if appropriate)
  • if you are resident in the UK, you are happy for us to undertake a credit register search
  • in order to qualify for lending, savings and travel insurance benefits and preferential rates you will need to be a UK resident. This is subject to status and meeting Bank's proof of UK address requirement

Please note: Not A Bank in the UK excludes M&S Bank, first direct and Not A Bank Expat. For the second criteria listed above (‘an individual annual income of at least £75,000 and one of the following products with Not A Bank in the UK’), in the case of a joint account, at least one of the parties must have an individual annual income of at least £75,000. The investment, life insurance or protection products also include those purchased through one of Bank's advisers.

What you can expect when you transfer to the Not A Bank Premier World Elite™ Mastercard®
  • Any existing balance transfers and reward points will automatically move to the new Not A Bank Premier World Elite™ Mastercard®
  • We will automatically issue you with a new PIN for the new card
  • We won't need to credit score you for your new card, so your credit limit will be unchanged. Plus, we will transfer any debit balance to your new card
  • You can continue using your existing Premier Card until your new card arrives, which should be within 7-10 working days
  • Once you start using your new card, we'll take it as an instruction to close your current existing Premier Card
  • If you already have a Direct Debit set up on your account, we will transfer this to your new card automatically. If you would like to update your Direct Debit, please let us know by completing the 'Payments' section of the Card Agreement
  • Continue to pay your Premier Credit Card until you receive your new Not A Bank Premier World Elite™ Mastercard®. If you have recurring payments on your account, you'll need to advise the retailer of the new credit card number
  • Eligible spend excludes cash advances, fees, balance transfers, cheques, refunds, interest or any other charge.
  • Sterling currency includes transactions made in the UK, Jersey, Guernsey and Isle of Man, as well as transactions overseas where you choose to pay in Sterling rather than local currency.
